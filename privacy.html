Ghosty Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Privacy-first by design

Ghosty ("we," "us," or "the App") is a privacy-first iOS application developed by Christian Festa LLC.

It filters unwanted connections on your device using custom domains you add (limited to 3 in the free tier, unlimited in premium) or curated blocklist categories you can toggle on/off. Premium unlocks viewing of all traffic logs (free tier shows only blocked entries) and unlimited custom domains. All features run locally on your iPhone or iPad.

We do not collect any information from you.

Ghosty collects zero personal data, device identifiers, usage statistics, crash reports, or any other information. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • All filtering happens locally on your device. No data about connections, blocked entries, custom domains, or traffic logs ever leaves your device or reaches our servers (or anyone else's).
  • Your added domains and toggle settings for curated categories are stored only in secure local storage on your device. Curated lists (if updated) are downloaded as public, non-personalized files with no user identifiers attached.
  • Traffic viewing: Premium users see full local traffic logs exclusively on-device. Free users see only blocked entries. These logs are never transmitted.
  • In-app purchases: Apple processes all subscriptions and payments. We receive no payment details, email addresses, or purchase-related personal information.
  • Analytics / tracking / third parties: The app uses no analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash reporters, or any third-party services that would collect or transmit data.

Data Storage

All your settings, custom lists, and local logs (if any) remain on your device using iOS secure storage. They are never backed up to iCloud unless you manually enable it for the app (under your full control). Deleting the app removes all local data.

Sharing or Selling Data

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data because we collect none.

Security

Your privacy is protected by design—nothing ever leaves your device. The app follows Apple's security guidelines and runs in Apple's sandboxed environment.

Children's Privacy

Ghosty is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly process data from anyone.

Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete. You fully control everything stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted in the app with a new effective date. Continued use of Ghosty after changes means you accept the updated policy.
